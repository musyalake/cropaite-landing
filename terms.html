4. Nature of the Cropaite Service

4.1 AI Diagnosis as a Decision-Support Tool

The Cropaite AI disease detection model is a decision-support tool. It is designed to assist farmers and agronomists in identifying crop diseases more quickly and consistently — it is not a substitute for the professional judgement of a qualified agronomist. The AI Diagnosis provided by the App is based on visual analysis of a single photograph at a single point in time, has a known margin of error, and should be cross-referenced with physical crop inspection and agronomist advice for high-value or export crops.

Important: Always cross-reference AI diagnoses with physical inspection and qualified agronomist advice before applying treatments to export or high-value crops.
