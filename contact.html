Common Questions

Frequently Asked

How do I join the pilot?

Fill in the contact form above selecting "Pilot Programme Enquiry" or send us an email directly at hello@cropaite.com. We'll schedule a short call or visit to get you onboarded.

Is the app really free?

Yes — completely free during Phase 1. We are selecting farms to test the system. In exchange for access, we ask for honest feedback to improve the platform.

What phone do I need?

Any Android smartphone running Android 8.0 or higher. No iOS version during Phase 1. You'll need to enable "install from unknown sources" to install the APK.

What crops does it support?

Currently optimised for floriculture (roses, carnations, chrysanthemums) and export vegetables. If you grow something different, tell us — your data helps expand coverage.

Is my farm data safe?

Yes. We store data on encrypted servers and never sell your data. Read our full Privacy Policy for details. You can request deletion of your data at any time.

What if the diagnosis is wrong?

The AI is a decision-support tool, not a replacement for an agronomist. Always cross-reference with physical inspection. Report any incorrect diagnosis through the app — it directly improves the model.
