What Cropaite Does

Precision scouting — for the field, not the office

Built for East African flower and vegetable farms. No agronomist on call required.

🔬

Instant Disease Detection

Photograph any affected plant and instantly identify the disease or pest — with severity level — in seconds.

💊

Actionable Treatment Guidance

Get specific chemical and organic treatment options with dosages — tailored to your crop and region.

🌦️

Local Weather Integration

Real-time farm weather helps you correlate conditions with disease risk and plan spray programmes smarter.

📋

Farm Health Records

Every diagnosis is logged automatically — build a reliable disease history across your fields over time.

🗺️

Multi-Field Dashboard

Manage all your fields from one place. Assign crops, monitor disease patterns, and track activity farm-wide.

🤖

AI Chat Assistant

Ask any crop question in plain language — get advice on prevention, disease cycles, and seasonal care.

Simple by Design

From photo to action in four steps

Designed for scouting teams in the field — no training required to get a diagnosis.

01 📸

Photograph the Plant

Open the app, tap Scan, and photograph the affected leaf, stem, or flower during your routine walk.

02 🤖

AI Analyses Instantly

The AI identifies the disease or pest, confidence level, and severity — in under 5 seconds.

03 💊

Receive Guidance

Get specific treatment options logged automatically to that field's health record.

04 📊

Track & Report

Review scan history, monitor disease patterns, and share findings with your agronomy team.
